About PDFLuxe

Welcome to PDFLuxe, the ultimate suite of free, secure, and lightning-fast PDF tools designed and developed by Yegireddy Pranay Kumar.

Our Mission

The internet is flooded with PDF tools that require you to upload your sensitive, personal, or corporate documents to unknown remote servers. PDFLuxe was built to change that. Our mission is to provide an enterprise-grade document manipulation tool fully within your web browser. No uploads, no waiting, no privacy risks.

How It Works

PDFLuxe leverages modern web technologies like WebAssembly (WASM) and advanced Javascript APIs (such as pdf-lib) to parse and process your files purely locally. When you click "Merge" or "Compress", your computer's CPU does the heavy lifting instantly. It's offline-capable, extraordinarily fast, and strictly secure.
