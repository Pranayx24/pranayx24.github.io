Advanced Tips & Best Practices

To get the absolute best results when compiling PDF documents, keep these best practices in mind:

  • Standardize Page Sizes: If you are merging PDFs with vastly different dimensions (like A4 and Letter size), the resulting file will maintain those distinct sizes. If consistency is required, adjust your documents beforehand.
  • Flatten Annotations: If your original PDFs contain interactive form fields or unflattened comments, they might behave unexpectedly after a merge. It is best to print to PDF first to flatten visual elements.
  • File Naming: Before you merge, rename your individual files with numbering (e.g., "01-Cover.pdf", "02-Body.pdf"). This helps you organize them much faster when uploading them into the grid.
