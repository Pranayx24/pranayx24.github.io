Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does it handle PDF images?

Because Markdown is a text-based format, embedded images cannot be directly inserted. The conversion focuses strictly on extracting and structuring the text content.

Are bold and italic styles preserved?

Yes, our parser attempts to detect font weights and styles, translating them into standard Markdown asterisks and formatting markers wherever possible.
