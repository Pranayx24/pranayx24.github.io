PDF to Word Converter

Convert your portable document format files into editable Microsoft Word documents securely. PDFLuxe uses entirely client-side JavaScript to scan the pages of your PDF and assemble the extracted text cleanly into a standard DOCX file.

Instead of relying on remote servers that store copies of your intellectual property and sensitive corporate files, PDFLuxe provides a robust text-extraction-to-DOCX script that runs solely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is saved.

✨ Premium Text Extraction

  • Instantly Editable: Extracts raw text natively while preserving paragraphs.
  • Zero File Uploads: Files are read securely inside your browser Sandbox.
  • Instant Formatting: Automatically maps PDF layout breaks securely into Word line breaks.
  • Completely Free: Export DOCX without expensive paid subscriptions.

⚙️ How to Convert to Word

  • 1 Upload PDF: Choose or drag your target text-based PDF file.
  • 2 Parse Data: Wait 1-2 seconds as the browser algorithm parses every text element.
  • 3 Download DOCX: Obtain an actively editable Word Document.

💎 The PDFLuxe Privacy Promise

Your privacy is absolute. Our PDF to Word conversion logic takes place entirely within Chrome, Safari, or Edge locally on your machine. This prevents sensitive resume details, legal invoices, or corporate playbooks from being cached on a server thousands of miles away.
