Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At PDFLuxe, your privacy is our primary concern. This Privacy Policy outlines our robust procedures concerning data handling and why we are fundamentally different from traditional online PDF platforms.

1. Absolute Local Processing

The core ethos of PDFLuxe is zero data transmission. When you use tools like Merge, Split, Compress, or Format Conversions on our website, your files never leave your device. All document processing occurs locally within your own web browser utilizing WebAssembly and advanced JavaScript.

2. No Server Uploads

Unlike other services that require you to upload your sensitive PDFs to remote servers, PDFLuxe does not have a backend server for file processing. We do not transmit, analyze, store, or have any access to the contents of your physical documents.

3. Local Storage and Cookies

We may use local browser storage (such as IndexedDB or LocalStorage) strictly to save your user preferences (like Dark/Light mode) or recent history state to enhance your user experience. We use standard analytical cookies (via Google Analytics) to monitor website traffic and ensure optimal performance.

4. Third-party Links

Our website may contain links to independent third-party websites. If you follow a link to any of these websites, please note that these websites have their own privacy policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please reach out to us at our Contact Page.

Your files stay on your device. 100% secure. No uploads.
