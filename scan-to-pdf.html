Scan to PDF Online Free

Need to digitize physical documents fast? PDFLuxe transforms your device's camera into a high-quality portable document scanner. Instantly snap photos of receipts, notes, or official documents and convert them into a shareable, multi-page PDF document completely free.

Whether you are on a mobile device or a desktop computer with a webcam, you can capture pages in real-time and construct your PDF immediately without the hassle of downloading bulky scanning apps.

✨ Premium Features

  • Works seamlessly on mobile: Access your phone's camera right from the browser.
  • 100% local processing: Scans happen on your device, ensuring total privacy.
  • Zero uploads: We do not store or transmit your sensitive documents.
  • Live preview: Confirm all page scans before producing the final PDF.

⚙️ How to Scan Documents to PDF

  • 1 Open the Scanner: Click "Start Camera" to grant secure access to your device's camera.
  • 2 Capture Pages: Snapshot one or more pages using the capture button. Review or retake instantly.
  • 3 Download PDF: Once all pages are captured, click "Create PDF" to instantly generate your file!
